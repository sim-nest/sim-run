# sim-cli

In one line: the terminal way in -- one `sim` command that starts a session, loads plug-ins, and answers you at a live prompt.

## What it gives you

Everything you need to drive SIM from a shell: the part that reads your command line and opens a session, the loaders that pull in outside plug-ins from files or bundles, an interactive prompt where you type a line and SIM answers back, and the library that draws it all in your terminal and reads your keystrokes.
